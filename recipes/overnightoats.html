Overnight Oats

Why Overnight Oats?!

I just can't get enough of harmless harvest plant-based coconut yogurts. I always wanted to make overnight oats for my lunchbox while im at work. So I searched this recipe online for this project.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup of old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup of plant-based milk
  • ½ cup of harmless harvest plant-based coconut yogurt
  • ¼ cup of mashed ripe banana
  • 2 Tablespoons of chia seeds
  • ¼ cup of chopped fruit (your choice)

How to craft the Overnight Oats

  1. In an airtight container, glass, or mason jar, add oats, milk, yogurt, banana, and chia seeds.
  2. Cover with lid or aluminum foil, and refrigerate overnight (or for 3+ hours).
  3. When you wake up, mix, top with chopped fruit, and enjoy chilled. No cooking or heating necessary—the oats “cook” themselves overnight.

Notes

  • Mix these up right in a mason jar to take them on the go.
